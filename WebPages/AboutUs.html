About Tyes

We are developing an improvement upon the 9-Hole Peg Test in order to extract more specific fine motor skill diagnostics. It is equipped with a printed circuit under a replaceable board, which comes with a variety of hole sizes and shapes. Sensors allow the device to record three types of data: the time to pick up a peg, the time to place it into a hole, and overall test time.

The data is displayed on the Dashboard as a detailed graphical breakdown of patient performance and progress from previous tests. From there the therapist can customize patient therapy to facilitate recovery.

With built-in sensors, the Tyes 9HPT shows patient progress online. Complete therapist assignments. Practice with games customized by you. Connect anywhere just by plugging it in. Recovery starts now.
